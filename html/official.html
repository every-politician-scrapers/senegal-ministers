The Institutions

The President of the Republic

The President is elected by direct, universal suffrage, by majority vote in two rounds.

The Government

The National Assembly

The Representative Assembly of the Republic of Senegal is called the National Assembly

Constitutional Council, Supreme Court, Court of Auditors, Courts and Tribunals

Constitutional Council, Supreme Court, Court of Auditors, Courts and Tribunals

The Economic, Social and Environmental Council

The Economic, Social, and Environmental Council is Senegal’s second-highest Constitutional Assembly.

High Council of Labour Relations

The High Council on Labor Relations is the national tripartite institution for social dialogue.

High Council of Local Authorities

The HCLA was established to support the territorial development process.
